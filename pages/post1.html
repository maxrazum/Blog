Oslo

12/18/2020

I think it seems like a great place to live - progressive and one of the wealthiest countries in the world.

Oslo is the smallest city between capitals in EU. It has around 600 000 inhabitants, which is really cool.

There are many parks, Oslofjord and a lot of forest around the city. People are generally nice, and there are a lot of opportunities for everybody.

Interested in making the move to Norway? You're not alone!

If you like the outdoors and nature, fishing, hiking, skiing, camping (even in winter), you'll like Norway. It's cold a big part of the year, but cold weather in Norway doesn't necessarily mean "bad" weather. There is a saying, "no such thing as bad weather, only bad clothing."

The natural beauty of the mountains, forests, lakes, fjords, seacoast and warmth of the people will make a life-long impression on you, I predict. Go during summer, see the midnight sun; during winter, the aurora lights.
